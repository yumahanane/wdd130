Overview

Purpose

The Whitewater Rafting Website has the purpose of marketing the sport, provide information on the company's offers, provide an easy online booking experience, and answer common questions. Through breathtaking images and videos and compelling quotes and narrative, the site will be marketing the sport. This will lead the audience to feel desirous of looking at the company's family friendly and affordable offers. Consequently, they will book their individual or group trips with the company.

Audience

Who are the target customers? What do they want? The target audience are families with children over 5 years old looking to create memories together, young professionals looking for an adventurous relaxation recreation, college students crossing out their bucket list during summer breaks, and young influencers looking for content. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They expect to find a range of options such as daily trips and multiple days trips, the prices for each option, and the equipment provided. They will also find answers to these questions: Are there any accomodations nearby? Why must they choose to book with the company? Is it family friendly? How should they prepare for it?
